Small Tattoos

 

There are so many different designs available when it comes to small tattoos. You can go with something simple like a flower or a butterfly, or you can go wild and try something more unique. Whatever you decide on, make sure that you pick a design that suits your personality.

To most people, size does matter. Whatever that means, now I’m talking about tattoos. Here we have provided you infos, ideas and designs to take inspiration from, especially when you want to get a tattoo and you have no idea what to get. In terms of sizes, we have provided different categories and ideas for placement.

If you like this tattoos you will also like lord shiva tattoos inked by Black Poison Tattoos.

Today, we give you small tattoo ideas and designs with positive meanings. This is especially good for tattoo first timers. If you’re scared that you wouldn’t be able to tolerate the pain, better start with small tattoos, and those who want to keep their tattoos simple or minimalist, a small one is suitable for you.

The beauty of tiny tattoos is that they are not too busy looking, and the details tend to be basic. The symbol or words will speak for themselves. My tattoo is a tiny design taken from a large and intricate traditional Indian henna pattern. It’s about an inch long and has faded comfortably into my heel in an uninstagrammable but beautifully weathered design. Getting a tattoo is a huge decision – there are undoubtedly emotional stages to it – but in the end, as long as you are happy with it, nothing else matters.
