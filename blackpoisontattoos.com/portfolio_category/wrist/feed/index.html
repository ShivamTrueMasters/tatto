Watercolor artwork can be done in many different ways:

  • Outline – A solid outline without any shading or highlighting will create an image that appears flat on your skin. This style can be used on its own or combined with other styles such as dotwork or linework for extra detail and dimensionality.
  • Dotwork – Similar in appearance to block color tattoos, but instead of using blocks of solid color, dots create patterns within each flower shape that create depth and shading effects just like paint would appear while being applied to the paper surface; this adds more dimensionality than simply outlining everything would provide alone since it separates each individual petal with its own layer instead just drawing one single line around them all together making them seem less connected together than they are.
